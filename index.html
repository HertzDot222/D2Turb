Single-frame atmospheric turbulence mitigation

D2Turb: Depth-Aware Simulation and Decoupled Learning

A physics-grounded synthesis protocol provides spatially varying degradation and intermediate tilt supervision, while a decoupled restoration pipeline uses Adaptive Structural Prior Injection (ASPI) to recover sharp texture and rectify non-rigid geometry.

Motivation

Depth changes the degradation

Real-world turbulence accumulates along the optical path: distant structures can be strongly blurred and warped while nearer regions remain relatively stable. D2Turb targets both texture recovery and geometric rectification under this spatial imbalance.

Core Idea

Depth-Aware Simulation meets decoupled restoration

01

Depth-Aware Simulation

A path-integrated depth map modulates blur and distortion strength and produces intermediate tilt supervision.

02

Decoupled Learning

A texture module estimates the tilt-only intermediate image before a rectifier predicts dense geometric unwarping.

03

ASPI

Adaptive Structural Prior Injection transfers structural cues from deblurring to the geometric rectifier.
